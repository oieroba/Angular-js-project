The interval unit in the list of Scheduled Tasks represents the frequency of task execution.M is monthly, w is weekly d is daily, h is hourly, and m indicates minutes. The interval count is a multiplier of frequency. For example, a minute task with an Interval Counts of 5 would be executed every 5 minutes.
